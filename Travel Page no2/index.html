Popular Destinations

Amritsar

It is home of the Golden Temple, one of Sikhism religion's most spiritually significant and most-visited gurudwaras. The city is also known for Amritsari food.

Jaipur

With its honeycomb-inspired palaces and towering forts, Jaipur is one of India's architectural marvels.

Odisha

Odisha is famous for its ancient temples, particularly those in the capital city, Bhubaneswar, known as the 'City of Temples'

Featured Activities

India Pakistan Border

The Wagah Border parade ceremony is a 30-minute event. The event begins with a parade. It's performed by soldiers of both countries

Hot air balloon

The hot air balloon activity is conducted from various locations in Jaipur. Amber Fort, Jal Mahal, Shiv Vilas and Delhi-Jaipur Road are some common locations. The site keeps on changing depending upon the direction of the wind.

Surfing at Puri Beach

Surfing at Puri Beach offers an exhilarating and unique experience, blending natural beauty, adventure, and cultural richness.
